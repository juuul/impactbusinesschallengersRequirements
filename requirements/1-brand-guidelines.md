# Brand Guidelines - Impact Business Challengers

## Primary Brand Color
- **HEX:** #3A79A7
- **Usage:** CTAs, headings, background accents

## Secondary Colors
- White: #FFFFFF
- Dark Gray: #1D1D1F
- Light Gray: #F4F5FA

## Typography
- **Recommended fonts:** Inter, Montserrat, Roboto
- **Headings:** Bold
- **Body text:** Dark gray for optimal readability

## Logo
- Logo must be visible on every web page
- Do not modify, rotate, or distort the logo
